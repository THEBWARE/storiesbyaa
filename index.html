School is Bad
By Alexandro Alvero Poem 1:
There once was a kid full of dread,
Who hated the school where hes led.
The rules make no sense,
The works too intense,
Id rather stay home in my bed! The teachers all talk and they shout,
They tell us what schools all about.
But math drills and spelling
Are endlessly dwelling,
I wish I could just sit this out. The lunch is a blob on my tray,
I poke it, then push it away.
It jiggles and shakes,
Who knows what it takes?
Id rather eat dirt any day. The homeworks a mountain so tall,
I stare at it, feeling so small.
Whats this even for?
Its just such a chore!
Id rather not do it at all. Recess is so short its unfair,
No time to run wild anywhere.
Just one little game,
Then back, what a shame
Its like they dont want us to care! So Ill dream of a world that is fun,
Where learning feels bright as the sun.
No rules to obey,
Wed play every day
Now thats how school really should run! Poem 2: None Are Millionaires
By Alexandro Alvero The teachers work hard every day,
Yet riches are far, far away.
No yachts or big homes,
No jets where they roam,
Just bills that they struggle to pay. The staff in the school do their best,
But life doesnt give them much rest.
No millions, no fame,
No wealth to their name,
Just jobs that are simple at best. They teach us to dream and to strive,
But none of them truly can thrive.
The lesson is clear:
They keep us in gear
Success feels like barely alive. Poem 3: School is good
By Alexandro Alvero
There once was a kid who could see,
That school was the place to be.
With books in his hand,
Hed learn and expand,
And dream of the things he could be. The teachers, they tried every day,
To guide him along the right way.
Theyd help him explore,
Unlock every door,
And teach him to think and to play. The lunch may not always delight,
But friends make it feel just right.
Theyd sit and theyd chat,
Laughing over this and that,
Sharing their joys with delight. Recess was a time full of cheer,
With games and good friends always near.
Theyd run, jump, and shout,
Let all the fun out
The best part of school every year! Homework might seem like a bore,
But it helped him to learn even more.
It taught him to try,
To reach for the sky,
And open his mind to explore. So heres to the joy school can bring,
A place where young minds learn to spring.
Its more than just books,
Its friendship and looks
At a future where all dreams can sing.
